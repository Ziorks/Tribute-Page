John "Spartan 117" Spartan (Master Chief)

Protector of the Galaxy

Here's a timeline of Master Chief's life and adventures:

  • 2511 - Born in Elysium City on the colony world of Eridanus II
  • 2517 - Studied by Dr. Halsey and Jacob Keyes and found to be intelligent and lucky
  • 2525 - Underwent the dangerous process of SPARTAN-II Augmentation Procedures that caused almost half of the Spartan candidates to die, or be physically disabled; John was one of 33 Spartans who made it through the process unscathed
  • 2552 - The Covenant Fleet of Valiant Prudence invaded Reach
  • 2553 - Lord Hood commissioned a memorial near Mount Kilimanjaro in Africa in honor of UNSC service members lost during the war
  • 2557 - Nearly five years after John went into cryo-sleep, the aft half of the Forward Unto Dawn entered orbit above Requiem, a Forerunner shield world

For more info on Master Chief and all his exploits check out Halopedia.
